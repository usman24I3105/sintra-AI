How?

We dont know exactly how yet. We just know the journey is long and the path is arduous. But we know that its important. And we know its all about the right team.

We stay brutally honest. Without open and honest conversations, creativity and everything stagnates and progress cannot flow.

Keep respect & gratitude. From our team, to our customers and nature. We try to stay humble and act with respect for all.

Do whats right. Our customers are mom and pop stores. Approach with care, love and simplicity. Humans are not just data-driven machines.

Work is play. Which means, have fun. Life is short. Focus on playing the best game you can given the rules of the game. Its never that serious.

Above all, we try to fuck around and find out. And work hard. And sometimes follow our gut. Were not perfect, but the more quality shots we fire, the closer we are to the truth.

Culture at Sintra

Thats why we try to hire the right people. We really want to know each human really well. Every person is either a great individual contributor or extremely knowledgeable in the field of expertise. We believe great people manage themselves.

Transparency. There is no number thats not shared with everyone on the team. This might not always be the most comfortable approach but we think its the right way.

Full ownership. That involves doing beyond whats written on the job title which might include design, user research, product or simply thanking the cleaner lady.

Work hard, move fast. Every second that were not serving our customers, someone might go out of business. Our work is our expression.

Leaps of faith. That sometimes means doing things that dont make sense or that dont seem rational. That require leaps of faith and following intuition.

We work with people, not just roles. Explore the many areas to contribute inside of Sintra.
