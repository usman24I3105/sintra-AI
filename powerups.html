Frequently asked questions

What are Power-Ups?

Power-Ups are specialized micro-AI tools within the Sintra App designed to automate specific tasks. They are tailored to each Helper's capabilities and allow users to complete tasks quickly with minimal input.

How do Power-Ups help streamline workflows?

Power-Ups simplify workflows by automating common tasks that would otherwise require manual effort. They enable users to generate content, manage data, and perform other tasks efficiently, reducing the time spent on routine activities.

How many Power-Ups are available in Sintra?

Sintra offers over 90+ Power-Ups, each designed to assist with different tasks across various Helpers.

Can anyone use Power-Ups in Sintra?

Power-Ups are available to users with an active Sintra Plan. Depending on the plan, users may have access to specific Helpers and their Power-Ups. Users with the Sintra X plan enjoy access to all Helpers and Power-Ups.

How do I use Power-Ups effectively?

To maximize the effectiveness of Power-Ups:
- Choose the appropriate Power-Up for your task.
- Fill in the necessary input fields with relevant information.
- Utilize the Custom Instructions field to specify tone, style, or other requirements.
- Review and refine the generated content as needed.
